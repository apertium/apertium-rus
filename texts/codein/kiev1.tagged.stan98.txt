  <div class="block-task-description">
    <p>The objective of this task is to disambiguate by hand 500 words of text in Russian. You can find a Wikipedia article you are interested in, or you can be assigned one, you will be given the output of a morphological analyser for Russian, and your task is to select the most adequate analysis in context. <br/>For further information and guidance on this task, you are encouraged to come to our <a href="http://wiki.apertium.org/wiki/IRC">IRC</a> channel.</p>
